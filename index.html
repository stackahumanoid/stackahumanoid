I'm an artist living in Croydon. I work in Ink, Watercolour and Acrylics, on paper.

I work in West London. For the last decade or so I've been commuting by bike into London from Croydon.

Over the last couple of years, to take my mind off work (which I was finding pretty stressful),and try and make better use of my pretty limited free time, I've committed more time to drawing and painting. I've always wanted to be able to draw and paint cars, so that's what I focused on.

Initially, I used images from magazines for reference. Magazine photography is great, but in my view doesn't make for interesting paintings. The presentation and subject matter is too glossy for my taste, I like things to look and feel a bit more raw, a bit more rough & ready.

Having a young family, I need to find ways to combine what I need to do every day (get to work on time), with what I'd like to do every day (ride my bike!).

I used to cycle over Battersea Bridge and through Chelsea, so would see some pretty tasty motors, Porsches, Astons, Ferraris... But again these don't interest me!
